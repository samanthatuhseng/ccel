Learning Objectives
  • Identify one’s root values regarding a systemic issue.
  • Understand why it is important to reflect on one’s own values in relation to a systemic issue.
  • Determine what counts as impact on a systemic issue given one’s own values.
  • Recognize that social context influences one’s values.
  • Empathize with others’ values.
  • Understand both the importance and limitations of individual action to achieve impact on a systemic issue.
  • Define ‘social impact’ or ‘collective impact.’
  • Understand the importance of finding value alignment (i.e., “common ground” or shared priorities) when trying to achieve collective impact on a systemic issue.
